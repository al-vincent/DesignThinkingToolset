What is snip?

Most of the best ideas don't start out as digital media - they start out with people in a room together,, sharing ideas, drawing on a white-board (and often with lots of sticky notes).

At some point though, it's often useful to turn those ideas into something that you can share digitally, like a slide deck. And that can take a long time to make.

snip is a simple little app that takes some of the work out of this process.
